Pie In The Sky

How To Play
The object of the game is to move around the board by correctly answering trivia questions. Questions are split into six colour-coded categories, and in order to win the game, a player must correctly answer a question from each category.
On their turn, a player rolls the die and can then move that number of squares to another point on the board, at which point they will receive a question related to the category of the square they landed on. If they answer correctly, they can repeat the process. If they answer incorrectly, their turn ends, and the next player starts their turn.
A player also has a maximum time of one minute at the board, before play automatically switches to the next player.
The game is won when a player has successfully answered at least one question from every category.
