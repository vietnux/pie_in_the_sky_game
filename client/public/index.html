Pie In The Sky

How To Play
The object of the game is to move around the board by correctly answering trivia questions. Questions are split into six colour-coded categories, which can be seen in the board legend.

On the player's turn, the die is rolled by clicking the "ROLL DIE" button and all possible moves are highlighted on the board. The player must select a given colour and a category related question will be displayed. If answered correctly, the player earns a slice of pie in their score. If you land on a category you have already earned a slice for, you will still have to answer correctly in order to proceed with your turn. If answered incorrectly, their turn ends, and the next player starts their turn.

A player also has a maximum time of one minute at the board, before play automatically switches to the next player. If the timer runs out and a question has not been answered, the next player will have the opportunity to answer the question and gain a slice of pie.
The game is won when all segments on the player's pie are full.

Congratulations!
